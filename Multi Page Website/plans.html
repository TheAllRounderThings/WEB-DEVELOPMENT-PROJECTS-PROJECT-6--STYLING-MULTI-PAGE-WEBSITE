PLANS

We have various plans to choose from for our customers. Based on the type of level you are and your requirements you can choose a suitable plan for yourself.

All the plans are just for one month and you can also choose from variety of cars available.

The pricing of the plans may differ with the features in various plans and the type of car chosen.

1. Basic Plan

If you choose the basic plan you will get a manual so that you can understand and remember the bsics of driving.

The Pricing of the basic plan depending on the type of car you choose:

i. For i10 Car: Rs. 600

ii. For i20 Car: Rs 700

iii. For Swift Car: Rs. 800

iv. For Honda City Car: Rs 850

2. Standard Plan

If you choose the Standard Plan you will get the manual that helps you understand and remember the basics of driving and you will also get to practise for two hours continously once a week in a month which means 4 times a month.

The Pricing of the Standard plan depending on the type of car you choose:

i. For i10 Car: Rs 900

ii. For i20 Car: Rs 1000

iii. For Swift Car: Rs 1050

iv. For Honda City Car: Rs 1100

3. Advanced Plan

If you choose the Advanced Plan you will get the manual that helps you understand and remember the basics of driving, you will also get to practise for two hours continously once a week in a month which means 4 times a month and you will get assistance from us till you completely get your Professional Driving License.

The Pricing of the Advanced plan depending on the type of car you choose:

i. For i10 Car: Rs 1500

ii. For i20 Car: Rs 1550

iii. For Swift Car: Rs 1600

iv. For Honda City Car: Rs 1700
